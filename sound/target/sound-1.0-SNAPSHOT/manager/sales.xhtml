Sound++

From this page the manager may search for any track or album in inventory to edit the sale price field of the item. Normally tracks and albums are sold for their list price and the sale price is zero. If an item has a sale price greater than zero, then it is considered on sale. If a search returns multi-ple results, then the manager can choose from the list of results. A sale price equal to or greater than the list price is not permitted.
