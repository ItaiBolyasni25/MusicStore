From this page a manager can request a number of reports on the inventory, orders, and clients. All reports accept a range of dates in addition to any details specific to the type of report. A report may just consist of a summary or as a detailed report. All sales report will show the total sales, total cost, and total profit. Any item that was valid during the report time frame but is now removed should indicate this fact. The required reports are:



Total Sales

Within the defined date range this report displays either the total sales for all items or displays a list of every item sold ordered by date in addition to the totals.



Sales by Client

For a chosen client and within the defined date range this report displays either the total sales or displays a list ordered by date of every item purchased in addition to the totals.



Sales by Artist

For a chosen artist and within the defined date range this report displays the total sales for all items or displays a list of every item sold in addition to the totals.



Sales by Track

For a chosen track and within the defined date range this report displays either the total sales and downloads or displays a list ordered by date of every purchase in addition to the totals.



Sales by Album

For a chosen album and within the defined date range this report displays either the total sales and downloads or displays a list ordered by date of every purchase in addition to the totals.



Top Sellers

Within the defined date range this report displays the inventory in order of total sales. Items that have never been sold are not included.



Top Clients

Within the defined date range this report displays a list of clients in order of value of their total purchases. Clients who have never made a purchase are not included.



Zero Tracks

Within the defined date range this report displays all tracks that have never sold.



Zero Clients

Within the defined date range this report displays all clients that have never made a purchase.
